Most commonly asked questions.

What is a chit fund and how does it work?

A chit fund is a type of savings scheme where a group of members contribute a fixed amount every month, and one member gets the collected sum through bidding or lottery each cycle.

Is investing in chit funds safe?

Registered chit funds under government regulations are generally safer. It is essential to choose a trustworthy and registered chit fund company.

What are the benefits of joining a chit fund?

Chit funds offer a combination of saving and borrowing. It can be helpful in emergencies, provide quick access to funds, and often have less strict eligibility criteria compared to banks.
